books

A book is a medium for recording information in the form off writing or images. typically composed of many pages (made of papyrus,parchment,vellum, or paper) bound together and protected by a cover. The technical term for this physical arrangement is codex. In the history of hand=held physical supports for extended written compositions or records, the codex replaces its predecessors, the scroll. a single sheet in a codex is a leaf and each side of a leaf is a page.
